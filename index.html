About Print$Buddy

Our Mission

No student should stress over printing. Print$Buddy provides fast, reliable, and affordable printing solutions right from MNIT Jaipur hostels, ensuring you meet every deadline with ease.

Our Story

Founded in 2025 by MNIT students, Print$Buddy started as a late-night printing solution for our peers. Today, we’re proud to serve the entire MNIT community with quality prints and delivery.

Why Choose Us?

We’re students, just like you, understanding the chaos of deadlines. With late-night hours, campus delivery, and a passion for quality, Print$Buddy is your trusted printing partner.

Community Commitment

We’re dedicated to supporting MNIT’s vibrant student community. From sponsoring club events to offering student discounts, Print$Buddy is more than just printing—it’s about building connections.
